L U M I N A R I U M

Luminarium is driven by the vision of Janez Grošelj, a light artist from Slovenia whose minimalist installations explore the captivating interplay of light and space. Inspired by the subtle wonders of nature and the cosmos, Luminarium transforms often-overlooked elements into mesmerizing experiences through light, aiming to enhance perception and emotional connection to environments.

Working with a team of experienced technicians and collaborating with diverse artists, Luminarium delivers astonishing experiences that have been showcased at numerous international festivals, including Lighting Guerrilla in Ljubljana, iLight in Singapore, BLINK Cincinnati in the USA, and Noor Riyadh in Saudi Arabia, among others.

Luminarium is also deeply engaged in the broader light art community. Working with the renowned Lighting Guerrilla Festival in Ljubljana, Luminarium carries forward a commitment to fostering emerging talent and pushing the boundaries of light art.

Beyond creating installations, Luminarium offers comprehensive solutions for organizing light festivals, providing a turn-key service for cities and clients seeking to illuminate spaces with impactful light art experiences.
